I am a PhD student in Computer Science at [University College London (UCL)](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/), supervised by [Prof. Chengxu Zhou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ZNeLJBgAAAAJ). I am a member of the [Humanoid Robotics Lab](https://ucl-humanoid.github.io/). My research focuses on **Humanoid robot locomotion and manipulation**, with a particular emphasis on reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and diffusion models for humanoid and quadruped robots.

Prior to UCL, I was a PhD researcher in Mechanical Engineering at the [University of Leeds](https://www.leeds.ac.uk/) (2023–2024), also supervised by Prof. Chengxu Zhou. I received my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the [University of Southern California (USC)](https://www.usc.edu/) in 2023, where I worked with [Prof. Quan Nguyen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=s5_69i0AAAAJ) at the Dynamic Robotics and Control Lab (DRCL) on trajectory optimization and model predictive control for legged robots. I hold a B.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from [Technological University Dublin](https://www.tudublin.ie/) (First-Class Honors) and [Nanjing Tech University](https://www.njtech.edu.cn/).

Research Interests
======
- **Legged Robot Locomotion:** Whole-body control, model predictive control, and sim-to-real transfer for bipedal and quadruped robots
- **Learning-Based Control:** Deep reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and adversarial motion priors for agile robot behaviors
- **Generative Models for Robotics:** Diffusion models for expressive humanoid motion generation and motion planning
- **Robot Manipulation:** Digital twins, dexterous robotic hands, and hand-object interaction
